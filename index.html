# Numerical Grade Letter Grade Grade Point Interpretation
1. 80% and above A+ 4.00 Outstanding
2. 75% to less than 80% A 3.75 Excellent
3. 70% to less than 75% A- 3.5 Very Good
4. 65% to less than 70% B+ 3.25 Good
5. 60% to less than 65% B 3.00 Satisfactory

7. 50% to less than 55% C+ 2.50 Average

9. 40% to less than 45% D 2.00 Poor
10. less than 40% F 0.00 Fail
